A company that is unique in the international broadcast arena, JWM enable broadcasters to maximise the true potential of their valuable promotional resource, be it TV, radio or digital services.


We work with broadcasters across the globe, on a project or on-going basis, to develop effective linear and non-linear promotional and break strategies, at both a portfolio and individual service level. Benefits for broadcasters include: Increased viewing & cross platform consumption, improved brand health, better efficiency of creative resource and increased commercial revenues (where relevant).
