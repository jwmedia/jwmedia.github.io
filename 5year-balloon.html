JWM 5 Year Anniversary April 2016 - Charity Balloon Competition

To celebrate their 5 year anniversary, James & Wilkinson Media launched 500 balloons from a grassy knoll in the English countryside for a special charity event. Each balloon had attached a ticket with instructions to the lucky finder. "A prize will be awarded to both the finder of the balloon that travels the greatest distance AND the first balloon that is received by us. Each prize is a £250 donation by James & Wilkinson Media to your chosen charity. Closing Date 22nd April 2016."
