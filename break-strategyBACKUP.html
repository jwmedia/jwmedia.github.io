Break Strategy to Maximise Audiences

Break and navigation strategy to address audience drop off, increase viewing across breaks and if relevant, maximise commercial impacts.


Service Benefits

  • Increase in audience viewing
  • Increase in advertising revenue
  • Increase in on-air marketing effectiveness
  • Improved viewing experience

The Issue

Shifting consumer habits means that broadcasters are coming under increased pressure to retain audiences across breaks. This reduces the ability to market content to consumers and can undermine commercial revneues and ultimately inheriting a poor viewing experience.


Scheduling and Marketing/Operations & Sales very rarely liaise in the creation and implementation for channel breaks, which leads to:


  • Sales and marketing inefficiencies
  • A negative viewing experience

We work with broadcasters to create a multi discipline formulation of channel break strategy and optimal approach across all business units. We beleive in shaping strategies that reflect the audience needs. We have a proven track record across various areas of break performance and discaplines, taking a best practice audience driven approach to improving breaks. This could include:

  • Navigational tools
  • Cross Platform navigation
  • benchmarking against competitors
  • maximising commercial rveneues
  • Recommending break structures
