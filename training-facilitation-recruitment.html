Training, Facilitation & Recruitment

Service benefits:

  • An Increase in effectiveness of marketing and media planning strategy and greater efficiency of promotional resource
  • Staff development and improved team integration and processes
  • A catalyst for senior management to make transformation across the wider business
  • Investment creates increase in self value of quite often unrecognised but critical staff

There is a lack of training in the broadcast market that is tailored specifically to the unique challenges that marketing strategy of broadcast promotion and its implementation provides. James&Wilkinson Media offer workshops intended to plug this gap with training specifically tailored to staff in all roles and at all levels whose roles include on-air marketing whether from a strategic or an implementation perspective.


We aim to equip attendees with the necessary knowledge, understanding and tools to create strategic, highly effective promotional campaigns within their organization or to implement them as relevant.


This service introduces commercial media planning practice into an organisation whilst maintaining editorial compliance and responsibility.


Possible subjects include:

  • Marketing strategy & prioritisation
  • Fundamentals of media planning; terminology, its calculation and application
  • Interpretation of marketing briefs, campaign objective setting, audience targeting, campaign planning and effective frequency calculation
  • Campaign implementation, junction/break construction, on-screen navigation and effective evaluation process
  • Strategies to increase retaining audiences in an ever increasing non-linear broadcast world
  • Guidance on broadcast management and optimisation systems

Recruitment

JWM offer advice on recruitment of media planning teams, including:

  • Creating an optimal media planning team structure
  • Assist with shaping job descriptions
  • Advise on salary expectations by benchmarking salaries within other broadcast media planning teams
  • aiding the interview process by shaping interview questions and providing media planning testing to verify existing media planning knowledge of any applicants

We have experience with large multi national broadcasters in this capacity over the last few years as they have gone through an internal restructure of their media planning teams so are fully equipped to work with broadcasters of any size.
