Digital and VOD Promotional Strategy

A diverse range of Digital, VOD and OTT projects to grow consumption of non-linear platforms. There are many challenges broadcasters often face when building a digital promotional strategy integrated within the goals of the business. These could include:


  • Siloed structures within organization with several data sources leading to an absence of central ownership
  • Little understanding of the potential value of an integrated linear and digital strategy
  • Limited marketing capability on non-linear platforms
  • Multiple brand consumer touchpoints without a central long term vision to address changes in audience viewing behaviour
  • Lack of control or budget to invest into large scale digital infrastrucutre


Our service benefits

  • Development of On-Platform VOD or OTT promotional strategy
  • Audit of VOD platform content promotion practices to benchmark & inform strategy
  • Development of marketing personalisation capability for VOD platforms
  • Development of targeted advertising capability to drive up revenues
  • Facilitation workshops to bring together key platform stakeholders to agree vision for marketing personalisation capability and shape roadmap to deliver this
