About us

Having launched in 2011, James&Wilkinson Media pride themselves on having the expertise to work with any broadcaster. This is emphasised by their diverse list of broadcast clients both national and international. Please hover over individual images below to find out more about the JWM team.

Alan James – Co Founder/ Director

  • Over 25 years experience in Media; encompassing Broadcasting and Advertising
  • Bringing best commercial practice to broadcast arena
  • The BBC’s first Head of Media Planning
  • Leading a team of 50 media planners, Alan was responsible for on-air media strategy and implementation for marketing across the corporation’s TV, radio and digital services
  • Broadcast Buying Director at WPP
  • Prominent speaker and chairman across the international conference scene, including Promax & EBU

Jo Wilkinson - Co Founder/ Director

  • 18 year media career that extends across BBC, SKY, Red Bee Media, Yell and various advertising agencies with a broad mix of roles, primarily in media strategy and planning
  • Unique and extensive knowledge of how to develop, manage and implement media planning practice within a broadcast environment
  • Hands on experience and expertise of channel rebrands, portfolio management, campaign planning and implementation, introducing navigational strategies and TV optimization systems

Joe Goddard - Media Planning Head

  • 10 Years of experience across broadcast industry within Marketing & Media Planning
  • Bringing International best practice and scientific approach to media planning at Nickelodeon UK
  • Extensive experience with various channel management systems & implementation of ratings driven optimization systems
  • Background in channel rebrands, campaign planning and prioritization models
  • Audience data analysis expertise, specifically within the children’s market
  • Creative advertising and graphic design background
